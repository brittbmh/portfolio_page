This app was my first Capstone Project at Prime. We were given two weeks to create a solo project. I created a party planning app that allows hosts to both provide details to their guests and to get the information they want back from the guests. It allows the host to select the questions that they want to ask, and then display the responses to those questions in a table. Guests are also able to log in to the app to track their invites and responses.

Tech Used: React.js, Node.js/Express.js, AJAX/Axios, Redux.js, Redux-Saga, PostgreSQL, Passport, Sweetalert.js, HTML, CSS

WeCAMP

For my group project at Prime I had the privilege to work on a team that developed a prototype app for a client. It was a great experience to work with a real client and help turn their ideas in to reality. I am proud of the work we accomplished in a short period.

WeCAMP is an app that parents can use to schedule and coordinate their child's summer. The user can search through a database of camps, organize their family's itinerary and share that information with other parents.

Tech Used: React.js, Node.js/Express.js, AJAX/Axios, Redux.js, Redux-Saga, PostgreSQL, Passport, Sweetalert2.js, HTML, CSS
